Road map

Stage 1: Launching Telegram Bot for Token Distribution

Goal: Attracting users and initiating community building.

Tasks:

  1. Development and Launch of the Telegram Bot
    • Define bot requirements and functionalities.
    • Develop the bot and integrate it with necessary APIs.
    • Test the bot thoroughly to ensure smooth operation.
    • Launch the bot and ensure it is accessible to users.
  2. Promotion of the Bot to Attract Subscribers
    • Create promotional materials (posts, videos, guides).
    • Share promotions on social media platforms and relevant forums.
    • Engage with influencers to help promote the bot.
    • Run targeted ad campaigns to reach potential users.
  3. Monitoring and Analysis of User Activity
    • Set up analytics tools to track user engagement and activity.
    • Collect feedback from users to identify any issues or areas for improvement.
    • Regularly review data to adjust strategies and improve user experience.

Stage 2: Token Exchange for NFTs with Gifts

Goal: Rewarding active users and increasing their engagement in the project.

Tasks:

  1. Development of the Token-to-NFT Exchange System
    • Design the exchange mechanism and user flow.
    • Develop the system for exchanging tokens for NFTs.
    • Conduct security audits to ensure the system's robustness.
  2. Preparation and Launch of the NFT Distribution Mechanism with Gifts
    • Create or acquire NFTs and define the gift structure.
    • Integrate the distribution mechanism with the existing platform.
    • Test the system to ensure smooth and fair distribution.
  3. Control over the Exchange Process and Distribution of NFTs
    • Monitor the exchange process to ensure it functions correctly.
    • Handle any technical issues or user concerns promptly.
    • Track distribution metrics and adjust the process as necessary.

Stage 3: Launching Alpha Version of Telegram Platform Application

Goal: Expanding functionality and attracting more users.

Tasks:

  1. Development and Testing of Coin Mining Features
    • Define the mining process and integrate it into the application.
    • Develop and test mining algorithms and features.
    • Ensure the mining process is secure and fair for all users.
  2. Implementation of Improvements and Additional Features in the Application
    • Collect user feedback to identify needed improvements.
    • Develop and integrate new features based on user needs.
    • Conduct thorough testing to ensure all new features work seamlessly.
  3. Marketing Campaign to Attract New Users
    • Create a comprehensive marketing plan targeting potential users.
    • Launch marketing campaigns across various channels.
    • Collaborate with partners and influencers to increase reach.

Stage 4: Market Entry with Gift Packs for Coin Burning

Goal: Increasing token liquidity and creating additional value for users.

Tasks:

  1. Development and Launch of Gift Packs
    • Design attractive gift packs that users can purchase.
    • Develop the system for coin burning and exchanging for gifts.
    • Launch gift packs and ensure they are easily accessible to users.
  2. Supporting the Process of Coin Burning and Exchanging Them for Gifts
    • Monitor the coin burning process to ensure accuracy.
    • Provide customer support to handle any issues or questions.
    • Track metrics to understand the impact on token liquidity.
  3. Monitoring User Reactions and Adjusting the Strategy as Needed
    • Collect and analyze user feedback on gift packs.
    • Make necessary adjustments to improve user satisfaction.
    • Continuously monitor market trends and adjust strategies accordingly.
